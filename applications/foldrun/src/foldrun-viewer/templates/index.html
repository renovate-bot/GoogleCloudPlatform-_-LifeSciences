How to Use

This viewer displays AlphaFold2 prediction results from Vertex AI pipelines. Access it through the FoldRun agent:

  • open_structure_viewer - Open viewer for a completed job
  • get_prediction_results - Get results and open viewer automatically

Features

The viewer provides multiple visualization modes:

  • Structure View: 3D protein structure with pLDDT coloring
  • Analysis Dashboard: Quality metrics, plots, and job metadata
  • Combined View: Side-by-side structure and analysis

pLDDT Confidence Bands

  • Very Low (<50): Low confidence
  • Low (50-70): Moderate confidence
  • Confident (70-90): Good confidence
  • Very High (>90): Excellent confidence

Data Handling

  • Protein sequences and prediction results are stored in your GCP project's Cloud Storage bucket.
  • Sequences are processed by Vertex AI Pipelines within your project.
  • AI-generated analysis uses Google Gemini via your project's Vertex AI endpoint.
  • No data is sent to external services outside of Google Cloud.
  • Data persists until you delete it using the agent's cleanup tools.
